Hi! I am a 4th year Ph.D. student in the School of Computing at KAIST. I am working with Juho Kim as part of KIXLAB.
My research interests in Human-Computer Interaction lie in video understanding and interaction. In particular, I leverage HCI and AI techniques to comprehend video content and build interactive technologies that empower users to create and consume video content more effectively.

Keywords: Human-AI Interaction; Video Interaction; Multimodal Understanding; Generative AIs
